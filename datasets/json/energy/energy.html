Tags: energy, renewable, united states, us, nuclear, fossil fuels, power

Overview

United States Government reports on consumption, production, import, and export of various fuel sources. This data comes from the US Energy Information Administration, which has historical data from 1949 to 2014. All numbers are given in Quadrillion BTUs.

http://www.eia.gov/tools/faqs/
