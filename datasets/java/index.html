Publishers

 From a newspaper article about analyzing amazon book sales by genre and publisher. Unfortunately, they do not have information on the book's title or author.

Finance

 The Annual Survey of State Government Finances provides a comprehensive summary of the annual survey findings for state governments, as well as data for individual states.
finance, government, expenditure, charges, money, united states, us

State Crime

 Records about the crime rates and totals for US states over time.
crime, burglary, larceny, motor, property, violent, assault, murder, rape, robbery

Supreme Court

 This dataset contains information about supreme court cases for the several decades.

Music

 The Music library is a compilation of over one million contemporary songs and information about their audio features and metadata.

 Records and computed statistics about the top 1000 books on Project Gutenberg.

Future Potential Datasets

The datasets listed below have not been fully implemented, but they could be! Feel free to consider them too.

  1. Agriculture: Datasets about agricultural statistics in the US (Link)
  2. Cancer: Incidences of different kinds of cancer in the US.
  3. Drug spending: Information about how much drugs cost to sell and produce.
  4. Food: Information about food regulated by the US.
  5. Foreign assistance: Reports of foreign assistance from the US to other countries.
  6. Hospitals: Information about hospitals across the US.
  7. Hydropower: Information about hydropower dams across the US.
  8. Mortgage: HMDA data provide information regarding home mortgage lending activity. The Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA) was enacted by Congress in 1975 and was implemented by the Federal Reserve Board's Regulation C. On July 21, 2011, the rule-writing authority of Regulation C was transferred to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB).
  9. New York Crime: Detailed information about crime reports in New York City.
  10. Officer Shootings: Reports about shootings committed by officers, including race.
  11. Religious groups: Information about membership in religious groups (Link)
  12. State Department per Diem: Information about per diem reported by US State Department employees.
  13. University crime: Reported statistics of crime on college campuses.
  14. Youth risk: The Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System (YRBSS) monitors six types of health-risk behaviors that contribute to the leading causes of death and disability among youth and adults, including: Behaviors that contribute to unintentional injuries and violence, Sexual behaviors that contribute to unintended pregnancy and sexually transmitted diseases, including HIV infection, Alcohol and other drug use, Tobacco use, Unhealthy dietary behaviors, Inadequate physical activity. YRBSS also measures the prevalence of obesity and asthma among youth and young adults.Link
